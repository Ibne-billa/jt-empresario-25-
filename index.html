Our Categories

About Empresario

This year, we present 8 exciting categories, offering students a chance to ignite their entrepreneurial instincts. These categories foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving. Delegates will be tested on analytical skills, innovation, confidence, persuasion, time management, and teamwork, while also facing physical challenges. JT Empresario unites the nation's brightest minds for a thrilling competition, providing a prestigious and dynamic experience.

Muse x Empresario

This year, like the previous one, Muse and Empresario, two of JT's most awaited events, are being held together. Though they will have different registrations and will operate as separate olympiads, they will play out on the same dates. Teams can perform in both Muse and Empresario to their liking. However, the award system for both events will be separate and delegates who only registered for one event can not participate in the other.

Our Mission

Empresario was created to address the need for quality business and entrepreneurial events, offering high school students a real-world glimpse into the business world. At LGS JT, we aim to expand young minds beyond the classroom by introducing them to entrepreneurship. This event ignites the entrepreneurial spirit within each individual, helping youth apply theoretical knowledge to real-world solutions for today's most pressing challenges.
